The Great Ricecracker Mystery!

In the early days, when the KFMH3 was young and innocent, there was a contest to select a design for the first ever KFMH3 T-shirt.

As all gathered around at the On-In, various works of art were exhibited. Some were unidentifiable, some too identifiable, many had forgotten to bring their designs so chose to exhibit regalia or body parts in their stead. But all were stunned at the revelation that we had an unknown genius in our midst. Other meager offerings were quickly forgotten as eyes feasted on the artistic labours of our very own Chinko Manko.

"Who is she"? wondered many. "Why does she have a ricecracker stuffed up her ass"? asked others. When questioned directly, our artist replied "It not ricecracker, it bunny tail". Artistic geniuses are sometimes guided by forces that not even they can fully understand.
