Scribe of Run #46 - February 11 - Mino

Hare: Lolita. Co-Hare: Imamura-kun. Runners: 10. Visitors: 2. (1 from Tokyo Ladies H3.)

Lost Love in the Mino Forest

Since love/lust has been in the air on the most recent KFMH3 On-On, this run, our Valentine Run, was described a as run that had something for everyone. For those like Wanda and Dr. Dog-Style for those who found love on the Hash! (Then, Wanda did not show up due to a wedding) For Triathon Bob and Snake Semen it was a run for those who are looking for love in all the wrong places! Or for Slimie Limie it was a run advertised as a run for those looking for adventure on the Hash! Lastly, it was dubbed as a run for GFL and CM, as a run for those looking for love/lust on the KFMH3 ........

About nine or ten tough KFM hashers were called into action on this bleak, snowy day. Did that worry CM? Not a chance. He promptly went into action and put together some paper hearts that were pasted to every hashers hind quarters. There were some newcomers to the Kinky Full Moon Hash on this run such as Slimie Limie who true to form and he decided to wear some very bright pants & a short T-shirt! Very Kinky! Also, there was Occupational Hazard (Yuki) and Tokyo Ladies Hasher called "Coming In Public" who decided to join the hash twice while in the Kansai. Out a sense of hospitality we decided not ask her how she got saddled with that name.

Despite a mild protest form some nameless observer, we waited for Dr Dog-style, Fumie and Triathon Bob who arrived about 30 minutes late. The pack started off somewhat together wound it way though the very step terrain. It a beautiful course set by Lolita and her boyfriend, Imamura-kun. Every so often the front runners Occupational Hazard and Slimey Limey kindly waited for those who were not so ambitious. Nevertheless everyone arrived at some famous waterfall. We came, talked some and Mooned the Moon! The tourist from the countryside were either amazed or horrified by the KFM hashers. We discovered a couple of monkeys who were willing to brave the cold, yet seem to have more sense than the this collection of hashers.

From that point the sun came out and warmed our spirits and bodies. The course was varied from very steep to extremely steep which challenged our hearts and quite long-perhaps 12 or 13Km. If there was any need to justify our actions on this cold day, the breathtaking scenery made it worthwhile. Some staggered to the goal about three hours later. And some even took a short cut on the last part of the course and made it home. And even three Hashers were lost from the rest of us! Many of us wondered out loud where could they be. The mood was one of a sense of urgency and concern. These hapless Hashers-Triathon Bob, CM and Coming In Public were quite lucky that CM had some money and they caught a bus to Mino Station. Many thanks for Imamura-kun and Gobshite who went back to look for these hasher. Also many thanks to Lolita and her boyfriend (he needs a Hash name!) for taking us through a challenging, but very beautiful course!

The On-On was at "OSHO", or the local Chinese greasy spoon", and lasted several hours until 9:30. However, it was a great time with the Hashers having some good conversation, food, and beer. "Unfortunately" (depending on your point of view!) there were no Downs-Downs and no Swing Low....but remember there are rules on the Hash!!!!!

The Quote of the Day: Frisbeetarianism: The belief that when you go on the HASH, your soul goes up on a mountain and gets stuck there.

Scribed by: GFL.

This instrument told us to go through Bantan-toll-road which was extremely crowded!!!!!! Welcome to the technology age! On-On!!!!!

After arriving "Sukebe-So", some people removed a lot of snow and the other people cooked pilaff & spaghetti for lunch. Most notable was Triathon Bob watching sports videos such as the Super Bowl. Sukebe who decided to go to cooking school from next May, and Mickey Mouse cooked spareribs with some vegetables. And of course, there was plenty of beer for everyone except Shinji, Snake Semen's son. On Sunday, the weather was warm and sunny and I found that Beerhead came in during the night. He drove all the way during after his KABUKI performance, drank some and fell asleep in a chair. In the morning, we skied together, abused each other while Triathon Bob read his SI magazine while waiting to get on the ski lift! Some things never change! Thank you Sukebe-san for the wonderful ski-weekend trip! ON-ON!!

Scribe of Run #49 - March 22 - Ikoma

Hare: Chinko Manko. Runners: 2.

In The Mountain Forest

Why didn't you come to this Wonderful Run?

Since love was in the air of Mino, CM decided to take us to hills of Ishikiri that overlooked the Nara/Ikoma area. It was dubbed as a run for those looking for adventure on the Hash! As Lugnuts told CM & GFL "he would come" on Saturday......and BT who was over heard uttering: "That's news to me"!

Apparently, they both "failed to come".....some lame excuse was used as "I have to go to a JALT Meeting I forgot about"! Snake Semen did not come because he claimed that he couldn't read the fine print anymore due to his advanced age! Dr. Dog-Style seems to be overtaken by a rare case puppy love in Kyushu, thus he has been left permanently weakened.

Anyway we took off to the start via a cable car that took us to the top of the mountain. GFL & Triathon Bob did most of the talking that seem to revolve around sports: Are the 49ers days numbered? The University of Minnesota basketball team chances seem good.... The Lakers lost again.....High Five!!!!

We slowly weaved our way down the mountain past a some very old temples and shrines. This run was far from the madness of the city and we probably only ran into four or five people on the trail. The view was breathtaking. About an hour later, we entered the urban area again, and we staggered to the goal dodging the traffic and then proceeded to hand out down-downs in the sunshine.

Also many thanks to CM, despite having a painful broken finger, for taking us through the most beautiful course I have had the opportunity to run. Let's do it again in the summer! A Great Run! A Great Day!! ON-ON!!
